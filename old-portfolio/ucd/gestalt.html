The Gestalt Principles

    There are seven gestalt principles in the UX:

  1. figure ground;
  2. similarity;
  3. proximity;
  4. common region;
  5. continuety;
  6. closure;
  7. focal point.

With these in mind I had to use the group's fidelity and recreate it using the gestalt principles.

The recreation

Again I am sorry for the picture, I will change this in the future update.

The left side is the fideltiy of our group and the right side is with the changes I made. The first thing I changed was the first section with the specialisations. With the principle 'continuety' I made the menu horizontal instead of vertical. I also pointed the arrows differentlh so that the user know how the box will work.

The second change is under that section with the examples. I used the gestalt principle: similarity. I placed the grey boxes and the text symmatrical.

The third change proximity. The grey boxes has their own width which differs both of them. This way the user wont think of them as one section, but instead seperate ones.
